#  Hospital Intelligence & Performance Analytics Dashboard

##  Project Overview

The **Hospital Intelligence & Performance Analytics Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using **PostgreSQL, Excel, Power Query, and Power BI**. The project transforms raw healthcare data into meaningful insights that help hospital management monitor operational performance, patient trends, and financial health.

The workflow begins with importing and organizing the dataset in **PostgreSQL**, where SQL queries were written to clean, transform, and prepare the data. The processed data was then imported into **Power BI**, where additional transformations were performed using **Power Query**, followed by data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive dashboard development.

---

#  Project Objective

Develop an interactive healthcare analytics dashboard that enables hospital executives to:

* Monitor hospital performance through key performance indicators (KPIs)
* Analyze patient demographics and treatment outcomes
* Evaluate financial performance and revenue drivers
* Identify high-performing departments
* Support data-driven strategic decision-making

#  Project Workflow

Downloaded the hospital dataset from Kaggle.
Imported the dataset into PostgreSQL.
Created database tables and wrote SQL queries to answer business questions and prepare the data.
Connected Power BI to PostgreSQL and imported the processed tables.
Performed additional data cleaning and transformation using Power Query.
Built relationships between tables and created calculated measures using DAX.
Designed interactive dashboards with KPIs, drill-through, bookmarks, and custom tooltips.
Generated business insights to support operational and financial decision-making.

---

#  Business Problem

Hospital management requires a centralized reporting solution to monitor operational efficiency, patient trends, and financial performance. Traditional reports make it difficult to identify revenue drivers, patient demographics, departmental performance, and treatment outcomes in a single view.

---

#  Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard is designed to answer key business questions, including:

* How is the hospital performing overall?
* Which departments generate the highest revenue?
* How has revenue changed over time?
* Which diagnoses contribute the highest patient volume?
* Which insurance types contribute the most revenue?
* How are patients distributed across age and gender?
* What are the most common admission and discharge outcomes?
* Which states generate the highest revenue?
* Does a longer patient stay increase revenue?

#  Walk Through of Key Insights

### Executive Overview

* Revenue generally follows patient volume, indicating admissions are a major revenue driver.
* Cardiovascular and Endocrine departments generate the highest revenue.
* Government subsidies and patient payments contribute almost equally to total revenue.

### Patient Analytics

* Patients aged **51–70** represent the largest patient group.
* Emergency admissions account for the majority of hospital admissions.
* Diabetes-related diagnoses generate the highest patient volume.
* Most patients recover successfully after treatment.

### Financial Analytics

* Revenue peaked in **2016** and **2021** before declining.
* Cardiovascular generates the highest departmental revenue.
* Revenue is influenced more by diagnosis than by longer patient stays.
* Maharashtra contributes the highest revenue among all states.
